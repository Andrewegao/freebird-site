Questions

Good to know

Is Freebird really free?

Yes. The app is free to download and use, supported by clearly labeled ads. We never charge for the feed and never sell your data.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There is no sign-in, no email, and no password. You open the app and the feed is there.

What happens to my birthday?

It stays on your device. We use it only to schedule local reminders before sign-up deadlines. It is never uploaded to a server, and it is not part of any account because there are no accounts.

Where do the freebies come from?

Real, nationally known programs — birthday rewards, brand sign-up bonuses, free samples, and national free days. Every entry shows when it was last verified, and we re-check links on a regular cadence so the feed stays current.

Are you affiliated with the brands you list?

No. Freebird is an independent aggregator. Brand names and logos identify the offer we found; they do not imply a partnership unless a card is explicitly marked sponsored.

How are the ads handled?

Ads are clearly labeled and use the same calm card style as everything else. There are no full-screen interstitials, and the claim moment is always ad-free.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone on iOS 17 and later.
